Our History

Al Sami was created in 2018 during a difficult time in my life. I grabbed my phone, hit record and unloaded every inch of myself. One hour later, with nothing left to say. I had released all of my emotions and made space for peace. I knew i had to share this amazing tool with the world and so I created the Al Sami listening app. Three years later Al Sami has expanded into Al Sami listening therapy that now provides one on one private listening therapy sessions.

To build mentally healthy people that promote a mentally healthy society.
